How to receive keys

  1. Go to your partners contacts and open the contact you want to share.
  2. Hold the two devices back to back (they have to be almost touching) and you’ll feel a vibration.
  3. After it vibrates you’ll see the content on your partners device turn into a card-like object with Star Trek warp speed-looking animation in the background.
  4. Tap the card and the content will then load on the your device.
